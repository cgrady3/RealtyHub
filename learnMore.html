Welcome To RealtyHub!

The Realty Hub business model at its core is a digital brokerage firm. Instead of a firm located in a single area, Realty Hub is primarily online which will help the company expand out to different cities and even states to reach as many agents as possible to do business through technology without feeling restricted. With the idea of implementing a complete computerized realty, it provides faster communication between the business and its clients providing great customer service which is a key point needed in realty. One of the advantages of the Realty Hub being digital is that money is being saved that would originally be used on outdated processes. The digital brokerage allows the company to work with a leaner and agile model as opposed to a more complicated one with more infrastructure while still reaching into the market. Also promoting the realty brokerage through technological formats such as social media and a proposed website will introduce the brokerage to a larger audience compared to just, for example, renting a billboard in a local area. This allows the company to reach a wider audience across all social media platforms. As of now in the business model the client Mr. Louie reaches out to clients through Google ads and has been in business since 2018. He does not keep a physical workspace but a digital one to do business with his realty agent clients and he provides them with a great offer of taking a lower percentage of their commission rate than that of the average realty brokerage firm.
