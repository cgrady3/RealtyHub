Welcome To RealtyHub!

The Realty Hub business model, at its core, is a digital brokerage firm. Instead of a firm located in a single area, Realty Hub is 100% online which allows our company expand out to different cities and states to reach as many agents as possible do business without phisically imposed restrictions. With the idea of implementing a complete computerized realty brokerage, it provides faster communication between us and our realtors and easier access to all of our resources.

Some of the other the advantages of the RealtyHub being digital is that the money saved by not paying for phisical office space which lowers our bottom line, savings which we can pass on to you! The digital brokerage model allows our company to work with a leaner and more agile model as opposed to the outdated physically limited infrastructure other brokerages still work with, while reaching farther among the market by being able to contract realtors working in different states in the south-east US. Promoting our company purely through technological formats such as the Google Ad you found us through, allows the company to be ubiquitous and reach a wider audience while it also saves the expense of doing physical advertisements as well.The long and short of it is, by not keeping a physical workspace but a digital one to do business with our realty agent, we can offer you a lower taking of the contracts we broker with you and your clients to increase your overall commission.
